## How INTPs learn

Effective Study Strategies:

- **Apply Knowledge**: Research real-world applications to make abstract concepts tangible.
- **Vary Perspectives**: Seek different viewpoints, even if group work is challenging, to deepen understanding.

## What Hinders INTP Learning:
- Rote memorization or **repetitive** tasks.
- **Trivial material** that doesn't deepen understanding.
- Rigid, overly structured group work or lectures without **flexibility**.
- Information presented without **logical flow**
